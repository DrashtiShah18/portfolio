# Consumer Sentiment Analysis for Pricing Strategy

## Overview

Designed and developed a multi-page Power BI dashboard to analyze customer sentiment, pricing effectiveness, and consumer behavior for Vitality Inc.'s magnetic bracelets. The dashboard transforms online customer reviews into actionable insights by combining sentiment analysis, pricing comparisons, and demographic trends to support data-driven business decisions.

**Project Type:** Marketing Analytics Dashboard

**Industry:** Health & Wellness Retail Analytics

**Tools Used:** Power BI • Power Query • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) • Microsoft Excel

---

## Business Problem

Businesses often rely on online customer reviews to evaluate product performance and customer satisfaction. However, while numerical ratings provide a general measure of customer experience, they do not fully capture the emotions, opinions, and concerns expressed in customer feedback. For Vitality Inc.'s magnetic bracelets, understanding how customer sentiment varied across different pricing tiers was essential to determining an effective pricing strategy.

This project analyzed consumer sentiment alongside customer ratings and demographic characteristics to identify how pricing influenced customer perception, satisfaction, and purchasing behavior. The insights generated support more informed pricing and marketing decisions by revealing the factors driving positive and negative customer experiences.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer sentiment across online product reviews.
- Evaluate the impact of pricing on customer satisfaction.
- Identify demographic trends influencing customer perception.
- Extract recurring themes from customer feedback using text analytics.
- Generate actionable recommendations to support pricing and marketing strategies.

---

## Dataset

The analysis utilized a dataset containing **270 customer reviews** collected over six months from **six online review platforms** for Vitality Inc.'s magnetic bracelets.

The dataset included:

- Customer review text
- Product ratings
- Sentiment scores and sentiment categories
- Price tiers ($10, $11.25, and $12)
- Customer demographics (Age Group, Gender, and Region)
- Product recommendation responses

The combination of structured and unstructured data enabled a comprehensive analysis of customer satisfaction, pricing effectiveness, and consumer behavior.

## Key Insights

- The **$10 price tier** received the highest customer ratings and the strongest positive sentiment, indicating that affordability played a significant role in customer satisfaction.
- Customer sentiment declined as prices increased, suggesting a disconnect between perceived value and customer expectations.
- Customers aged **21–44** generated the majority of reviews and expressed comparatively higher levels of negative sentiment.
- Positive reviews commonly highlighted **comfort, relief, affordability,** and **effectiveness**, while negative reviews frequently referenced **disappointment**, **poor performance**, and **product quality concerns**.
- Customer ratings and sentiment scores showed a generally positive correlation, while recommendation analysis identified opportunities to improve customer advocacy.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were identified:

- Optimize pricing strategies to better align with customer-perceived value.
- Strengthen marketing messaging by emphasizing comfort, relief, and affordability.
- Set realistic customer expectations regarding product benefits.
- Continuously monitor customer sentiment to evaluate pricing and marketing effectiveness.
