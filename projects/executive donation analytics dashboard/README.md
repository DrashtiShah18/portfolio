# Nonprofit Donation & Membership Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

Designed and developed a multi-page Power BI analytics solution for a nonprofit organization to monitor donations, member engagement, fundraising performance, and regional trends. The dashboards provide role-specific insights to support data-driven decision-making.

**Project Type:** Business Intelligence Dashboard

**Industry:** Nonprofit Analytics

**Tools:** Power BI • Power Query • DAX • Excel

---

## Business Problem

The organization required a centralized analytics solution to help leadership monitor fundraising performance, understand member engagement, identify regional trends, and support strategic decision-making. The dashboards were designed to answer key business questions, including:

- Which regions contribute the highest donations?
- Which members donate the most?
- How do donations change over time?

---

## My Role

I completed the entire analytics workflow:

- Cleaned and transformed raw datasets using Power Query
- Combined multiple datasets into a single data model
- Built relationships between tables
- Created interactive Power BI dashboards
- Designed role-specific dashboards for executive and departmental stakeholders
- Generated business insights and recommendations

---

## Methodology

The project followed a complete business intelligence workflow:

- Data preparation and transformation using Power Query
- Data modeling and relationship creation
- KPI and measure development using DAX
- Dashboard design tailored to different business stakeholders
- Business insight generation and strategic recommendations
